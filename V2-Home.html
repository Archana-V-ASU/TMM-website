About Us

We've been championing endurance, inclusivity, and the unstoppable spirit of the marathon
for over two decades. Learn more about us here!

Since its inception in 2004, TMM has grown into Asia's largest and most prestigious road race and one of the top ten marathons in the world, renowned for launching India's running movement and raising hundreds of crores for charity.
