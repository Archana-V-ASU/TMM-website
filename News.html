TMM 2025 Reaffirms the Power of Sport in
Driving Social Change

Mumbai, 17th April 2025: As the Tata Mumbai Marathon (TMM) draws the curtain on its landmark 20th edition, it continues to stand as a shining example of how sport can drive social change.

What began as a vision to inspire and unite communities has now evolved into India’s most impactful sporting movement.

With support from 275 NGOs, TMM 2025 raised INR 63.62 crores for social causes — reinforcing the spirit of #ChangeBeginsHere.

— Asarah Shaikh

Tata Mumbai Marathon Recognized By World Athletics For
Sustainable Best Practices

After recently achieving the 'Athletics for a Better World' Recognition by World Athletics, this recognition further reflects the Tata Mumbai Marathon's ongoing commitment to creating a positive environmental and social legacy, inspiring change beyond the race itself.

A heartfelt thank you to all our partners, sponsors, volunteers, and participants for their unwavering support in making TMM not only a world-class running event but also a catalyst for community well-being and environmental stewardship. Together, we continue to champion the spirit of running for a better future.

The Tata Mumbai Marathon (TMM) has been honored by World Athletics in its Athletics for a Better World Standard report, being recognized for best practices in two key sustainability categories – Zero Waste and Food Donation.

With strong stakeholder engagement and careful planning, the Tata Mumbai Marathon diverted 96% of its waste from landfill. TMM donated more than 10,000 pieces of fruit to the Roti Foundation, which was distributed to underprivileged school children and cancer patients across the city.
